# Benchmark Minerals Intelligence

Interactive pricing dashboard for critical minerals and precious metals used in battery and EV supply chains.

## Features
- Daily price assessments for lithium, cobalt, nickel, gold, silver, platinum and more
- OHLC exchange pricing data (COMEX, LBMA, LME)
- Market indices tracking battery raw material prices
- Supply chain data including mining operations and cell manufacturers
- Interactive Streamlit dashboard with filtering and visualizations

## Data Tables
- **BATTERY_CRITICAL_MINERALS_PRICING** - Daily price assessments and OHLC data
- **MARKET_INDICES** - Benchmark market indices (base-100 from Jan 2017)
- **BATTERY_SUPPLY_CHAIN** - Supply chain operations and pricing
- **MINING_OPERATIONS** - Global mining operations details
- **MINERAL_PRICE_ASSESSMENTS** - Detailed price assessments by grade and region
- **SUPPLY_DEMAND_FORECASTS** - 5-year supply/demand outlook
- **BATTERY_CELL_MANUFACTURERS** - Global battery cell manufacturing plants
- **ANALYST_REPORT_CHUNKS** - Chunked analyst reports for search

## Getting Started
Open the app to launch the interactive Streamlit dashboard.
Access data tables via the CORE schema.
